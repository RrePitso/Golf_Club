A Historic Course on the Ocean's Edge

Founded over a century ago, West Bank Golf Club is one of only three courses in South Africa with a working lighthouse in its midst. Whether you are battling the coastal winds on our signature 17th Par 3 or enjoying the view from the 19th hole, the ocean is your constant companion.
